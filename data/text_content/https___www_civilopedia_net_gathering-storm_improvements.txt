Introduction - Improvements and Routes - Civilopedia - Civilization VI
Introduction
Improvements can increase the output of your tiles (such as
Production,
Gold, and
Food). They also provide access to special bonuses provided by certain resources. If you do not improve your land, your civilization will almost certainly be overwhelmed by more established civilizations.

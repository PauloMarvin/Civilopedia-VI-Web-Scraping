Convert to Coal Power
Description
Decommissions the current Power Plant in this city, and replaces it with the Coal Power Plant.
Coal Power Plant:
Automatically converts any amount of
Coal into
Power for cities within 6 tiles that need it each turn, at the rate of:
1
Coal
4
Power
Heavy CO2 into the atmosphere

District
Industrial Zone

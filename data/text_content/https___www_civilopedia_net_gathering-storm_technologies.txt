Introduction - Technologies - Civilopedia - Civilization VI
Introduction
Advancing technology makes a civilization stronger, bigger, smarter, and a formidable opponent. It is critically important for a civilization to keep up technologically with its neighbors. While it is possible for a unenlightened civilization to overcome their more advanced neighbor, there are only a few examples of such events occurring in history –so too is this the case in Civilization VI.

Recommission Nuclear Reactor
Description
Allows for the recommissioning of a nuclear reactor to increase safety measures and reduce the risk of a nuclear accident.
Recommissioning resets the reactor age to 0. The older a reactor gets, the higher the chance that a Nuclear Accident can occur.

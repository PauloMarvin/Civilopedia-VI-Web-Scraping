Carbon Recapture
Description
Can be run in any city with an Industrial Zone. When complete, the lifetime carbon emissions of this civilization are reduced by 50 and 30
Diplomatic Favor are earned. This project can even reduce your carbon debt to zero and allow you to accumulate reductions against the global CO2 footprint.

Earning Faith
Earning Faith
Faith is the currency of Religion and can be earned from wonders, policies, leader abilities, natural wonders, city-states, Beliefs,
Trade Routes, and of course the Holy Site district and its buildings. Look through the Civilopedia to find sources of
Faith.

Great Prophets
Great Prophets
You may found your Religion once you have earned (or purchased) a
Great Prophet. Religious buildings will accumulate the
Great Prophet points necessary to bring this special unit to your civilization. After the
Great Prophet has been earned, send the unit to your Holy Site, and then found your Religion.
Great Prophets may also be earned from Stonehenge.

Introduction - City-States - Civilopedia - Civilization VI
Introduction
City-states are smaller political entities that cannot win the game, but can greatly assist or impede a civilization's progress towards victory. There are a few options to deal with these city-states. You can befriend them and gain a number of important benefits, you can ignore them, or you can conquer them.
